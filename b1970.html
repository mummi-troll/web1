Moominvalley In November (1970)

In her last, most profound and poignant Moomin story, Tove Jansson explores themes of loss, legacy and hope. The Moomins have left their beloved Moom- invalley but as winter draws near Snufkin, Mymble, Toft and others move into the Moominhouse to await the family's return. Could that gentle flicker of light on the horizon be their boat?
