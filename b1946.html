Comet In Moomin Land (1946)

In Comet in Moominland Moomintroll and Sniff set off to consult the professors in the distant Lonely Mountains. Their journey is packed with adventures and they meet new friends on the way - Snufkin, the carefree wanderer, the fascinating Snorkmaiden, and her brother the Snork. They discover that a fiery comet is heading straight for their beloved Moominvalley. But can they race home before the comet crashes?
